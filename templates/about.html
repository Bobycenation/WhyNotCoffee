Our Story

One day in 2017, a statement came out to create a coffee shop based on the routine activity we do every day, namely looking for coffee shops. A simple statement why not to make a coffee shop that is of course good quality.

The simple thought just for us to create a place to gather and drink coffee while having simple conversations grew into our interest in a philosophy of coffee that could unite us. From there, our belief was born that making a coffee shop must be very serious, so we created a coffee shop with specialty coffee.

Thus, Whynot Coffee Specialty Coffee was born, which started from a simple place that offers the best quality coffee and of course healthy for daily consumption.

Visi & Misi

Visi

WhyNotCoffee memiliki visi untuk mampu memperkenalkan ke berbagai banyak tempat bahwa menikmati kopi yang berkualitas tidak harus selalu mahal dan mengenalkan bahwa kopi lokal indonesia dan kopi international mampu berkombinasi dengan baik memberika rasa yang terbaik.

Misi

WhyNotCoffee memiliki misi untuk membuka bebeberapa cabang guna mampu mewujudkan visi kami. Tentu juga terus meningkatkan kualitas kopi dengan upgrade tools kami dengan baik.
